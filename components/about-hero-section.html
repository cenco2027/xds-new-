Our Story

XDS designs, engineers, and manufactures bicycles at global scale—with over 8 million bikes produced annually in our vertically integrated facilities. Every frame, weld, and assembly is built in-house, using advanced automation and precision testing to ensure consistency, performance, and long-term value.

From Olympic athletes to daily commuters, our bikes are trusted by riders around the world—because we don't just build for volume. We build for quality, efficiency, and the ride itself.
